History

School History

B/Keppetipola Maha Vidyalaya, one of the oldest schools in Keppetipola town, will go down in history. Since the school began, the primary section was one of its divisions. Based on student population, regional needs, and the policies of the Ministry of Education, arrangements were made in 2012 to separate and operate a distinct primary school section. As B/Vidyaloka Primary School, it has become a popular school where thousands of children from the area have been educated since 2012 to date. Day by day the number of children attending continues to grow, and currently close to 800 students attend the school. Scholarship results also improve year by year.

By achieving outstanding victories in national-level extracurricular competitions and examinations, it has brought glory not only to the school but also to the country. Having become a shining school in the region in a short period since its founding, it is believed that it will continue to grow and reach even greater heights.
